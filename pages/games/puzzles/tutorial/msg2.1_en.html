Some vehicles such as crab can be used to gather ore or transport other units.


Move your crab near freighter to take pontoon a northwest passage:
  1. Bring your crab near freighter.
  2. Transfer pontoon from freighter to crab.
  3. Move the crab near the estuary created by the low tide
  4. Finally, unload the pontoon on the single hex sea that separates you from the other side
