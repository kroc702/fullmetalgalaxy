Every action costs action points (AP) which increase every turn or over time depending on the game mode
AP, tides and some other information are grouped in the top of screen.

Get out the weather hen which predicts future tides and build units:

  1. Click on your freighter, then on your weather hen.
  2. Click on an adjacent hexagon. (the cost of this action is displayed)
  3. Confirm action by clicking the OK button or again on the same adjacent hexagon.
